Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Job Junction

Account Setup

How do I create an account? ▼
Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in your details, and verify your email to create an account.
I forgot my password. What should I do? ▼
Click on "Forgot Password?" at login, enter your email, and follow the instructions to reset it.

Job Applications

How can I apply for jobs? ▼
Visit the job listings page, select a job, and click the "Apply" button. Fill in the required details and upload your resume.
Can I track my job applications? ▼
Yes, go to your profile dashboard to see the status of your applications.

Internships

Are internships paid? ▼
Some internships are paid, while others may be unpaid or offer stipends. Details are mentioned in each listing.

Employers

How do employers post jobs? ▼
Employers can register, log in, and use the "Post a Job" option to list openings.
