Privacy Policy

At Job Junction, your privacy is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, resume, and profile details you provide.
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, searches performed, job applications submitted, and interactions.
  • Device & Log Information: IP address, browser type, device type, and activity logs.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Used to enhance user experience and analyze trends.

How We Use Information

  • To personalize and enhance your experience.
  • To send updates, newsletters, and job alerts (with consent).
  • To analyze usage patterns for improvement.
  • To ensure security and compliance with laws.

Data Protection

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Third-Party Services

We may use analytics, hosting, or other services, each with their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at support@jobjunction.com.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
